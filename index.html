Story

Everything you need to know about the Apex Predator:

Ancient cave paintings

There were numerous 200'000'000 Years old cave paintings found across the globe, indicating a previous co-existence of Godzilla and Humans. They often portrayed clashes with different titans. There's even one painting showing ancient civilizations praying to Godzilla, seeing a God in him.

Hiroshima, 1945

After hibernating for hundreds of millions of years, the nuclear bomb dropped on Hiroshima at the end of WWII is what made Godzilla wake up again. As the humans started to notice his presence, they tried to kill him with nuclear bombs. These attempts to kill him were covered up with the explanation that it was "just" bomb testing. All that, while not knowing that Godzilla actually feeds off radiation.

Present Battles

In the modern day, Godzilla is battling off many titans that have awakaned alongside with him. Starting in the Year 2014, he faced off against MUTOs (Top left), 2019 he faced off against the three-headed dragon Ghidorah (Bottom middle) and most recently, in 2021 (film took place in 2024) he faced off against the mighty Kong (Top right).

Opponents

There are many mighty titans. But only one can rule all of them.

He was never supposed to be on planet earth. A false king.

King Ghidorah is a three-headed dragon with alien origin. He fell on planet earth hundreds of millions of years ago, civilizations back then mistaking him for a meteorite. Due to him not being from earth-nature he reacts differently to certain environmental changes. While you mostly see him in the air with his gigantic wings, he is actually taller than Godzilla when he properly stands up for once.

A fearless fighter, knows nothing but being King.

Kong is a 100m tall gorilla. Having absolutely no fear and respect for anyone, it makes him a force to be reckoned with. In fights, he has the same advantage as humans. He can use weapons. While all other titans primarly only fight with their body, Kong is capable of ripping out a massive tree and fight with it in one hand.

The human-made titan. Not equal, but superior.

Mechagodzilla is fully made out of the strongest steel and metal, making him unable to experience any pain at all. It's fully equipped with gigantic weapons, capable of dealing unbelievable damage or adding force to his punches. Apex Cybernetics, the creators of Mechagodzilla have also made an expiditon to the hollow earth, gathering the source of titan-power. This made Mechagodzilla capable of firing an extremely powerful beam of his mouth (red), similar to Godzilla's atomic breath (blue).
